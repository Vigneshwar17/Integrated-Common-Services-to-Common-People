Our Services

Aadhar and Land Services

Aadhaar Seva Kendras (ASKs) provide streamlined Aadhaar enrollment and update services. Land Service Providing offices, or Tehsil/Taluk offices, manage land records and facilitate property transactions. They ensure accurate land ownership records, transparency in dealings, and efficient land administration, empowering citizens with secure land tenure and essential land-related services.

Government Schemes

The Indian government offers diverse schemes to support students, women, and farmers. Educational scholarships empower students, while Beti Bachao Beti Padhao and MUDRA Yojana foster female education and entrepreneurship. Farmers benefit from financial stability and crop insurance through PM-KISAN and Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana, ensuring sustainable agricultural development and security.
